About the Technology

RetinaAI uses the state-of-the-art U-Net++ (Nested U-Net) architecture for medical image segmentation. This advanced deep learning model features nested skip connections and deep supervision, achieving exceptional performance on retinal vessel segmentation tasks.

Trained on the DRIVE dataset with 20 high-resolution fundus images, our model achieves 96.08% pixel accuracy and 83.82% Dice coefficient, making it suitable for clinical applications in diabetic retinopathy screening, retinal disease diagnosis, and automated ophthalmological analysis.
